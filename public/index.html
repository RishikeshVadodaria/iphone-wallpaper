🔧 How It Works

  1. Design wallpapers in Illustrator → export as PNG
  2. Upload to iCloud Drive or a Photos album
  3. Create iOS Shortcuts with "Set Wallpaper" action
  4. Add automation triggers (time, location, low power)
  5. Disable "Ask Before Running" → runs silently ✨
